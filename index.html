Welcome to Farmer J's!

A corn maze is a great experience for families, couples, church groups, school groups, birthday parties, senior groups, or anyone just looking to have fun! There will be corn cannons, pumpkins, huge slides, and concession stands! Don't miss it this year!

Group rates start with 25 or more people. No coupons with group rates. We will accommodate groups or parties at special reserved times other than regular hours.

Tables and Chairs available for your comfort, parties or picnicking

This year’s mazes will be fun and challenging for all ages!

 

Family Friendly!
 

We are a family friendly corn maze, which means we do not haunt the maze. However, you are welcome to come after dark and bring your flashlight. There are maps and way points to help you make the maze as hard or as easy as you like.

If you have any questions, please feel free to Contact Us.
